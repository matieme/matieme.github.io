01
OUTSIDER
UNITY / C# / 3DSMAX
Outsider was developed as the final project for my video game design and programming degree. Developing this prototype took a year, and it mutated from a puzzle game to an action game with brief puzzle moments. The final project for this degree consists of creating a game with a group of people, where each member takes care of a different aspect of the game (in this case I was in charge of the game design). After finishing the course of studies, we presented the game idea and prototype in the AGS (Argentina Game Show) which is a big gaming festival in the country. In this instance, we refactored the combat system and revamped the art. Modelling, texturing and the animation of the new assets were other things I was in charge of for the AGS prototype.

Ghost Roads
UNITY / C#
Ghost roads is a project I did for a final of a subject during my degree in DaVinci. This game consists of creating a compulsion loop through the character's movement, who is a ghost, and avoid colliding against the walls or the obstacles, trying to get as far as possible without losing. The game was developed in two weeks in Unity and C#.
